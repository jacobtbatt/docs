title: 'How Do I Access My Admin Dashboard?'
description: 'Best practices, advice, and guidance for team administrators'

If you already have an admin account on Thnks you can find your admin dashboard by logging into app.thnks.com and navigating to Analytics (top right corner of page) > Admin. This data will populate in real-time to show you your team's giving activity.
